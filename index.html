About me

Hi! I am a postdoc research fellow at the National University of Singapore, where I am a member of the NExT++ Lab, working with Professor Chua Tat-Seng. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Technology of Tsinghua University, advised by Professor Zhiyuan Liu at the Tsinghua Natural Language Processing Lab. Before I became a Ph.D. student, I also received my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University. My research interests include natural language processing, multimodal large language models and information extraction.
